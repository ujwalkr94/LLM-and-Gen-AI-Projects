Outline for Poem about Dogs

1. Introduction
	- General affection for dogs
	- Brief mention of their companionship

2. Characteristics of Dogs
	- Loyalty and unconditional love
	- Playfulness and energy
	- Protective nature

3. Symbolism and Meaning
	- Symbol of loyalty
	- Representation of joy and happiness

4. Personal Reflection
	- Personal experiences with dogs
	- Emotional bonding

5. Conclusion
	- Gratitude for their presence in our lives
	- Eternal bond between humans and dogs
