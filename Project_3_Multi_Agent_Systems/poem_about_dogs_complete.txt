   A. Reflection on the joy they bring
   B. Final tribute to their unwavering companionship

Poem:

In the gentle gaze of loyal eyes, we find,
A friendship pure, unlike any kind.
From wagging tails and joyful barks,
To quiet nights with gentle snores.

Oh, playful spirits of boundless glee,
Chasing the wind, so wild, so free.
Curiosity glows in every glance,
In life's grand, infinite dance.

At home, they guard with silent might,
Guiding the lost, with the softest light.
Working, playing, always near,
In every setting, they bring cheer.

Through stormy days and endless nights,
Their presence a balm, a soothing delight.
Stories of courage, stories untold,
Their bravery is a sight to behold.

So here’s to companions, steadfast and true,
A lifetime of joy, given anew.
In their paws, we find sweet rhyme,
Unwavering friends throughout all time.
